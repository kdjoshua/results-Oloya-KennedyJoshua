The zenj Leadership Special Session


Leadership Training
The Training takes 1 week, and each session is 45 minutes. During the Training, Facilitators at Zenj Leadership cover practical subjects such as;
  1. Discovering your Vision.
  2. Designing your Mission .
  3. Applying your Vision and Mission in all you do daily.
  4. Relevance of SMART GOALS.
  5. Significance of Working with a system of Effective Habits.
  6. Mastering the Art of Decision making for Vision realization.
  7. Understanding of what servant Leadership is.
